Facts and Stories

3

At the close of the song 'Sticky,' audio can be heard of fashion designer and close associate of Drake's Virgil Abloh. Abloh died in November 2021, following a two year battle with cancer. As well as namedropping Abloh the lyrics to this track, Drake dedicated his album Honestly, Nevermind (2022) to his late friend.

The beat for 'Sticky' began when Australian songwriter RY X sent Gordo an MP3 of his vocals. Gordo created the melody for the instrumental by chopping up the vocal sample, all while he was on a flight from Dallas to his home in Toronto.
